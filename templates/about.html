About Maharashtra Lok Kalyankari Seva Sanstha

Who We Are

Maharashtra Lok Kalyankari Seva Sanstha is a registered social organization established in 2006. The organization works for the welfare and development of society through educational, social, cultural and sports activities.

The NGO is dedicated to creating positive social impact by supporting community development, spreading awareness, encouraging education and promoting public welfare.

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, social awareness, healthcare support and welfare initiatives that improve the quality of life for all sections of society.

Our Vision

To build a progressive, educated and self-reliant society where every individual has equal opportunities for growth, dignity and development.

Our Objectives

  • Promote education and skill development.
  • Support healthcare and awareness programs.
  • Encourage cultural and sports activities.
  • Work for community welfare and social development.
  • Assist underprivileged sections of society.
